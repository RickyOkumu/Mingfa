Who We Are

MINGFA CBO is a community-based social enterprise in Nangina Ward, Busia County, Kenya, focused on eco-friendly fish farming, environmental conservation, youth empowerment, and gender equality. Through innovative aquaculture and community-driven projects, we foster sustainability, food security, and green job creation.

Our Key Programs

Fish Farming

Training youth in sustainable aquaculture and backyard pond creation.

Financial Literacy

Empowering youth groups with savings, investment and entrepreneurship skills.

Girl Excel Program

Mentorship and life skills for girls to reduce GBV and increase empowerment.
